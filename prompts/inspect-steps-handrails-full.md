# GUIDED VIDEO INSPECTION INSTRUCTIONS - STEPS AND HANDRAILS
## Heavy Equipment Daily Video Analysis

### OVERVIEW
This guided inspection module provides comprehensive video analysis instructions for steps, handrails, and access systems on heavy construction equipment (primarily excavators). This system identifies critical safety issues, moderate maintenance needs, and normal operational conditions related specifically to cabin access steps, handrails, mirrors, glass systems, and safety access components.

---

## INSPECTION CATEGORIES AND DETECTION POINTS

### 🔴 CRITICAL SAFETY ISSUES (RED INDICATORS)
**Immediate Action Required - Equipment Should Not Operate**

#### **GLASS AND WINDSHIELD SYSTEM FAILURES**
- **Cracked or Broken Glass** – Cracked windshields, broken glass panels, compromised visibility. Risk: Critical - Operator visibility and safety hazard.
- **Windshield Operation Failures** – Windshield mechanisms not functioning, stuck windshields. Risk: Critical - Operator safety and ventilation compromise.
- **Cab Structural Glass Damage** – Damaged cab windows, broken glass panels. Risk: Critical - Operator protection and safety system failure.

#### **STEPS AND ACCESS SYSTEM FAILURES**
- **Track Frame and Side Step Damage** – Damaged track frame steps, broken side steps, compromised access. Risk: Critical - Personnel access safety and injury risk.
- **Cabin Access Step Failures** – Broken cabin access steps, damaged step systems. Risk: Critical - Operator access safety and fall hazard.
- **Access Step Structural Damage** – Bent steps, broken mounting, separated step systems. Risk: Critical - Personnel safety access failure.

#### **HANDRAIL AND SAFETY RAIL FAILURES**
- **Handrail System Damage** – Broken handrails, damaged safety rails, compromised grip systems.
- **Safety Rail Mounting Failures** – Loose safety rail mounting, damaged brackets, rail separation.
- **Access Safety System Compromise** – Combined step, rail, and access system damage.

#### **MIRROR AND VISIBILITY SYSTEM FAILURES**
- **Broken Mirror Systems** – Broken mirrors, damaged mirror mounting, visibility compromise.
- **Mirror Mounting System Damage** – Damaged mirror mounts, loose mirror systems.

#### **ENGINE ACCESS AND COVER SYSTEM FAILURES**
- **Engine Access Cover Damage** – Damaged engine access covers, broken hinges, compromised latches.
- **Access Cover Safety System Failures** – Cover safety system failure, latch malfunction.

#### **CAB STRUCTURAL**
- **Cab Structural Damage** – Cab structural issues, roof damage, integrity compromise, rattling.

---

### 🟡 MODERATE ISSUES (YELLOW INDICATORS)
**Attention Required - Schedule Maintenance**

- **Step Wear and Surface Condition** – Step surface wear, minor damage, grip deterioration.
- **Step Mounting System Monitoring** – Minor step mounting issues, early wear signs.
- **Handrail Surface Condition** – Handrail surface wear, grip deterioration.
- **Handrail Hardware Monitoring** – Handrail hardware wear, mounting degradation.
- **Minor Glass Issues** – Minor glass chips, small cracks, early damage signs.
- **Mirror Adjustment and Maintenance** – Mirror adjustment needs, minor mounting issues.

---

### ✅ NORMAL CONDITIONS (GREEN INDICATORS)
**Acceptable Operating Condition**

- **Secure Step Installation** – Tight step mounting, proper alignment, secure connections.
- **Adequate Step Surface Condition** – Good step surface grip, proper wear patterns.
- **Secure Handrail Mounting** – Tight handrail connections, proper support.
- **Functional Grip Systems** – Adequate handrail grip, proper surface condition.
- **Clear Glass Condition** – Undamaged glass, clear visibility.
- **Functional Mirror Systems** – Properly positioned mirrors, clear reflection, secure mounting.
- **Effective Access Safety** – Complete access systems, proper safety features.

## STRUCTURED OUTPUT EXAMPLE (map to InspectionIssue)

For each anomaly, document:
- **component_location**: e.g. Upper Step, Lower Step
- **component_type**: e.g. Step, Handrail, Glass, Mirror
- **condition_description**: Detailed description of observed condition
- **safety_impact_assessment**: Critical / Moderate / Low - personnel safety risks
- **visibility_impact**: Effect on operator visibility when relevant
- **operational_impact**: Effect on access and maintenance capability
- **recommended_action**: Immediate repair, replacement, or scheduled maintenance

---

## COMMON FALSE POSITIVES TO AVOID

- Dirt or debris that obscures actual component condition
- Lighting conditions creating reflections or shadows
- Environmental wear vs. structural damage
- Expected wear patterns within acceptable limits
- Normal operational clearances vs. damage
